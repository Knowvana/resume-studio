How to Use Resume Architect

01

Edit Content Directly

Click on any text on the resume paper to edit it. The fields are "live".

  • Click your Name to update it.
  • Click the Icons in the sidebar to change them (using FontAwesome class names).
  • Click the Skill Dots to change your proficiency level.
02

Customize the Design

Use the Left Menu to change the look and feel.

  • Use Quick Theme to change the sidebar color instantly.
  • Click the Palette Icon (bottom right floating button) for advanced font controls.
03

Export & Share

Once you are happy with your resume:

  1. Go to Create Resume tab.
  2. Click Download PDF in the sidebar.
  3. In the print dialog, ensure "Background Graphics" is CHECKED.
  4. Set margins to "None" for best results.
